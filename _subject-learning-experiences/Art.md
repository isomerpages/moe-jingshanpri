<h3>Every JingShanite an active artist and informed audience.</h3>
<p>The Art Curriculum enables every child to enjoy art, communicate visually
and make meaning. It provides a progressive and age-appropriate education
for creative and innovative aesthetic self-expression, while developing
students’ range of artistic interests and talents.</p>
<p>In order to develop JingShanites into active artists and informed audiences,
the curriculum aims to develop students’:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Visual inquiry skills to explore their surroundings</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Curiosity, imagination and enjoyment in Art making and discussion</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confidence in working individually and in groups to create artworks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Awareness of and respect for histories and cultures of artworks and artists
in Singapore and the world</p>
</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Core Art Learning Experiences</strong>
</p>
<p>At Jing Shan, art is a core subject and lessons are conducted once a week
from Primary 1 to 6. In alignment with MOE’s Art Syllabus, all students
will go through three core learning experiences of the art curriculum</p>
<table style="minWidth: 50px">
<colgroup>
<col>
<col>
</colgroup>
<tbody>
<tr>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Drawing</strong>
</p>
</th>
<th rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Through the use of various materials and engaging in different processes
such as scribbling, doodling, and sketching. The drawing experience develops
self-confidence and joy of using art to convey ideas.</p>
</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Museum-Based Learning (MBL) at Primary 4</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>MBL is designed to provide the learning of local art in an authentic context.
Primary 4 students will visit the museum and participate in a range of
inquiry and learning activities.</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Exhibition in Curriculum</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Students display their own artworks in the classroom as part of the lesson.
Through that, students learn to communicate about art.</p>
</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>

<p><strong>Signature JS Art Experiences</strong>
</p>
<p>On top of the Core Art Learning Experiences,&nbsp;students will also have
opportunities to experience, acquire and inquire on art through the following
signature programmes.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Termly Art Exhibitions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Termly Aesthetics Assembly</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Art Enrichment Programme (Stop Animation) at Primary 3</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pottery Programme at Primary 4 and 5</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>JS Arts Festival</p>
</li>
</ul>
